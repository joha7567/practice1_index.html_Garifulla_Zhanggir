  2. s1mple

        Oleksandr "s1mple" Kostyliev (born October 2, 1997) is a Ukrainian professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player who currently plays for Natus Vincere as an AWPer. He is widely considered as one of the best CS:GO players in the world.
    S1mple's first team was a team called LAN DODGERS, but he was soon signed by Courage Gaming. Around September 2014, s1mple was signed by a large organisation known as Hellraisers. S1mple was soon after signed by Flipsid3 Tactics, but his time in the team would cut short due to the team's semi-final exit at ESWC 2015. Contrary to his teammates, s1mple wasn't happy about the result. At this period in his career, s1mple was known for being quite "toxic," or rude to his teammates. After a short stint studying Chinese at a university, s1mple moved to America in early 2016 and joined Team Liquid. After leaving Team Liquid, he replaced Danylo "Zeus" Teslenko on Natus Vincere. With Na'Vi, s1mple won ESL One: New York 2016, and he was awarded the #4 placement in the HLTV top 20. Due to s1mple's personal performance, he would be nominated for the # 1 spot at the HLTV top 20 players of 2018. According to the statistics from HLTV, s1mple in 2018 had achieved the highest individual rating out of all previous top 20 players. He is considered one of the best players to ever play Counter-Strike.Link to inst and page on hltv









  3. device

        Nicolai "dev1ce" Reedtz (formerly known as devve and device) (born September 8, 1995) is a Danish professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and former professional Counter-Strike: Source player who currently plays for Astralis as the AWPer. He is known as one of the best players of all time, consistently playing at a very high individual level since 2014.
    The brightest representative of the young Counter-Strike school in Denmark. Despite his age, for several years he has been one of the best players on the planet and the most promising player in Denmark. He began his career in Counter-Strike: Source, where he did not achieve anything significant. In mid-2012 I switched to CS: GO. In recent years, he has defended the colors of such organizations as Copenhagen Wolves, Team Dignitas and Team SoloMid. In almost all of his teams, he was with dupreeh, who is considered Nikolai's best friend. In early 2016, the device, along with its former TSM partners, secured sponsorship and they launched a new organization, Astralis. In 2015 he was named the best esports personality in Denmark. Every year it is included in the top 10 best players in the world by reputable publications and portals. Link to inst andpage on hltv






  4. NiKo

        Nikola "NiKo" Kovač (formerly known as NiKolinho and nIKOLINHO) (born February 16, 1997) is a Bosnian professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive and former professional Counter-Strike player who currently plays for G2 Esports. He is also the cousin of huNter-. NiKo is widely considered as one of the most mechanically gifted players in CS:GO and has been in contention for the title of the best player in the world at multiple periods throughout his career.
    NiKo is Bosnia and Herzegovina's main young star after pita. He started his career in CS 1.6, but did not manage to achieve anything significant. With the transition to CS: GO, he played exclusively for Balkan teams such as iNation, GamePub and aimface, where he stood out very much among his teammates. This was noticed by mousesports, and in April 2015 the Bosnian was invited to join mouz. However, two months later, Nicola was moved to a replacement position. During this time, he managed to help in the role of replacing Team Kinguin at the qualifications of Gaming Paradise, as well as SK Gaming at IEM Gamescom. Nikola's excellent performance at these tournaments has had a positive impact on mouz's management, and since the end of August 2015, he has established himself at the heart of the mouz. Only in February 2017, NiKo changed his team, moving to FaZe Clan. And only recently he joined G2. Link to inst andpage on hltv





  5. electronic

        Denis "electronic" Sharipov (born September 2, 1998) is a Russian professional Counter-Strike: Global Offensive player, who currently plays for Natus Vincere. electronic began his professional career in mid-2015 as he joined the Evolution squad. After that, he played for three teams, Rebels, Team Empire and NokSuKao, from January to September 2016. There, electronic was able to showcase his individual talents and because of that got noticed by FlipSid3's captain, B1ad3, who subsequently invited him to replace Shara on the roster. Under his guidance, electronic stepped up his performance to a whole new level, which eventually led to him joining Natus Vincere in Denis "seized" Kostin’s stead.
